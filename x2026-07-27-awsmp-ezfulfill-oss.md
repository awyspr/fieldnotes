# awsmp-ezfulfill oss

2026-07-27

## What's this one all about then ?

One thing that early stage AWS SaaS ISVs in particular find tricky is how to get integrated fulfillment and provisioning
to work on AWS Marketplace. After seeing a subscription request from a public or private offer, there's specific requirements to register customer entitlements, and then to capture the additional data thats needed to provision the service. Most people do this once and never think about it again, but you have to make that one time work.

We've been asked to help with this a fair number of times and we've put together an open source project [``awsmp-ezfulfill``](https://github.com/awyspr/awsmp-ezfulfill) which implements whats required as middleware, and perhaps the basis
for an ISV building out further functionality as their market presence grows.

https://docs.aws.amazon.com/marketplace/latest/userguide/saas-product-customer-setup.html
